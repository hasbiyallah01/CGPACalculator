# CGPA Calculator

A simple Java application to calculate your Cumulative Grade Point Average. Just add your courses and get your results!

## What it does

- Calculates your CGPA based on your course grades
- Requires 18-24 units (standard semester load)
- Shows all your courses in a table so you can see everything
- Lets you edit or remove courses easily
- Won't calculate until you have complete information

## How to run it

Just double-click `start.bat` and the calculator will open automatically.

## What you need to do

1. Add your courses (need 18-24 units total)
2. Fill in course name, units, and grade for each
3. If you're a continuing student, add your current CGPA and cumulative units
4. Click calculate when everything is filled in

## Using the calculator

**Adding courses:**

- Type the course name
- Pick how many units (1-6)
- Select your grade (A, B, C, D, E, F)
- Click "Add Course"

**Managing your courses:**

- See all courses in the table
- Click a course to edit it
- Remove courses you don't need
- Clear everything to start over

**For continuing students:**

- Enter your current CGPA and total units from previous semesters
- Leave blank if you're a new student

**Getting results:**

- The calculate button turns on when you have everything filled
- You'll see your GPA, CGPA, and grade classification

## Requirements

- Need between 18-24 units total (standard semester)
- Each course needs a name, units (1-6), and grade
- Course names must be different
- If you enter current CGPA, you must also enter cumulative units

## Grade classifications

- **4.50 - 5.00**: First Class
- **3.50 - 4.49**: Second Class Upper
- **2.50 - 3.49**: Second Class Lower
- **1.50 - 2.49**: Third Class
- **0.00 - 1.49**: Fail

## Notes

- The calculator saves your courses automatically
- You can't calculate until you have complete information (this prevents mistakes)
- Works best with 18-24 units per semester
